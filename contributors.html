All financial contributors to the PGXN effort will be listed in perpetuity here on the “Contributors” page of PGXN.org. In addition, all contributors will be invited to a PGXN Launch Party at an appropriate PostgreSQL conference, depending on the timing of the PGXN launch.

Founders

Many thanks to our amazing “Founder” level contributors, whose logos and links are displayed prominently on the PGXN home page, and will be listed in the press release when PGXN launches. Founders will also receive a PGXN t-shirt at the launch of PGXN.

Payment by Check, Purchase Order, or Wire
